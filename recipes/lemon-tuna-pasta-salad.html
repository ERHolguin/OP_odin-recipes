Lemon Tuna Pasta Salad

Lemon Tuna Pasta Salad

A classic tuna pasta salad with a twist!

Prep Time:

30 mins

Cook Time:

15 mins

Servings:

6

Ingredients

  • 8oz elbow macaroni
  • 1 5oz can tuna
  • 1 tsp coarse kosher salt
  • 1 tbsp coarse kosher salt (yes, we need 2 different portions)
  • Juice of 1/2 lemon
  • 1/2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise

Utensils

  • 2.5 Qt sauce pan
  • Strainer
  • 8.5-cup food storage container
  • Fork
  • Can opener

Steps

  1. Add water to the sauce pan until 75% full. Then, add 1 tbs of salt and 1 tbs of olive oil. Bring water to a boil.
  2. Once water starts to boil, pour in the pasta, turn the heat down to medium and cook for 15 mins.
  3. Turn off the heat and strain the pasta. Follow with a shower of cold water to stop the cooking process.
  4. Pour the pasta in the storage food container.
  5. Open the can of tuna and drain it. Add the tuna to the pasta one forkful at a time and mix well after each portion.
  6. Sprinkle 1 tsp of salt over the mixture, followed by the mayonnaise. Then, mix with the fork.
  7. Close the lid of the container and shake until everything is well combined.
  8. Open the container once more and add the lemon juice. Close, then it shake it again.
  9. Refrigerate until ready to serve. Serve cold.
